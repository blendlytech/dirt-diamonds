**Load-bearing design decisions:**
- **Two hard boundaries** keep the arc calibration-safe: (1) the social/economic stats (GPA, reputation, social_status, attractiveness, charisma, …) NEVER touch a baseball rating — they feed events/dating/opportunities/parents only, so the whole phone+marketplace+dating surface is calibration-inert; (2) **exactly three** person stats reach the sim, only at HS-6, only capped+zero-centered: `teamwork`/`confidence`/`happiness` — and **none of the three is item-buyable**, so money can't buy on-field performance through this layer (that lane stays `Player_Equipment`'s). ⇒ HS-1…HS-5 add ZERO calibration risk; the MLB bit-identity guard must stay byte-exact through all of them; HS-6 is the single planned re-band.
- **Person stats are STICKY, not needs** (§2.2): they don't auto-decay toward 0; they change only when an action/event moves them. Only two mild exceptions, both HS-4: `gpa` (the one true weekly drift model, closed form = f(school-attendance, intelligence, discipline, study, partner, stress), neutral inputs ⇒ drift 0) and `happiness` (weak mean-reversion to a setpoint). No `NeedsEngine`-style tick needed for the layer.
- **Alignment derived from `morality`** at thresholds (Good ≥67 / Troubled ≤33), never stored — matches the schema comment.
- **Stored base vs. effective** for the three status stats: `effective = clamp(base + Σ item modifiers, 0,100)`, computed at read, never written back (the `EquipmentLedger` "computed, not written" discipline) — an item loss reverts cleanly.
- **Wealth tiers (§3):** 5 tiers, frequencies 12/28/35/18/7 %, starting funds 50/200/500/1,500/5,000 — **tier-2 (modal) funds = $500 = the shipped flat `CareerManager.StartingFunds`**, so a typical avatar is economically unchanged and the 8a economy stays where it was tuned. Home Wi-Fi only tiers 2–4 (spec's "poorest have no home internet"). Strictness rolled INDEPENDENTLY of wealth (orthogonal narrative axis). Transport = owning a category-Transport `Player_Items` entry (no transport table); tiers 3–4 gift a car / tier 2 a bike / tiers 0–1 self-buy for a one-time +work_ethic/+discipline/+maturity reward the gifted never get (spec §4.5).
- **Phone economy (§4):** hardware tier gates FEATURES (1 burner=Messages+Bank, 2 adds Marketplace, 3 adds Social/Contacts); plan+minutes METERS usage (0 prepaid $10→100min / 1 basic 50min-wk refill / 2 unlimited bypasses all). Wi-Fi (home if `home_wifi`, or a free-Wi-Fi location action costing a time block) is free. **HARD INVARIANT: answering/resolving a pending gritty-event thread costs 0 minutes, always** — minutes gate only player-INITIATED actions; no minutes ⇒ pending events fall to autopilot (forfeit precedent), dispatcher can never stall. HS-3 must keep the two paths physically separate.
- **Item catalog (§5):** `Assets/Data/Items/items.json`, validated loudly at load (not an FK). Entry = id/name/category/price/modifiers/transport_hours_saved/autobuy_min_tier. `modifiers` buff STATUS stats only (attractiveness/social_status/reputation), summed with a per-stat aggregate cap `ItemBuffCap`=+15, computed not persisted. Since those stats don't feed the sim, the item system is invisible to `run_monte_carlo_batch` (clean inverse of equipment: gear→ratings/never-status, items→status/never-ratings).
- **Zero-at-50 modifier (§6, the HS-6 contract):** `PointsFor(s,cap)=clamp(round_away(cap·(s−50)/50), −cap,+cap)` — `PointsFor(50,·)=0` by contract (all-50 ⇒ every effective rating bit-identical ⇒ bit-identity guard byte-exact, the `TierEffects`/`EquipmentEffects` identity precedent). Mapping: confidence→bat_power/pit_stuff, happiness→bat_contact/pit_control, teamwork→team defense; **cap ±2 (tunable to ±3)** ≈ ±0.04–0.06σ on the 50-pts/σ scale (smaller than equipment's +6). `bat_discipline`/`pit_stamina` untouched; `discipline`→command a RESERVED deferred lever. Slots into the effective chain at Initialize/roster-load before the per-PA modifiers; neutral fast-path resolves through the exact pre-HS-6 call.

At maturity/takeover `NurtureBlend.Apply` folds accumulated nurture into the fixed `HeirGenetics` nature baseline — zero-centered, round-away, clamped: coaching+funding−neglect → Potential (cap ±8), care−neglect → interest (cap ±20). **Nurture identity contract: neutral axes (50/50/50/0) ⇒ deltas 0 ⇒ final = pure nature**, so "no `Child_Development` row" ≡ "neutral row" ≡ pure-nature heir (matches the v11 schema comment; existing heirs/NPC children untouched, heir fixtures stay valid).
