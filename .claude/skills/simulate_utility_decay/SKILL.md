# simulate_utility_decay

**Purpose:** Balances the life simulation aspects (The Sims elements).

**Execution Details:**
When invoked, simulate 168 in-game hours (one week) of passive Need decay (Hunger, Sleep, Fitness, Social, Hygiene) for a standard NPC and output a text-based graph of their states.
This helps tune the base decay rate so players aren't starving to death after a 3-hour baseball game.
